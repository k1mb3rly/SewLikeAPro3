Part 4: Quick Reference Checklist

Everything to complete before the next class, in order.

Before Next Class

  • Finish tracing and editing all pattern pieces
  • Cut all pieces (main fabric, lining, interfacing)
  • Fuse all interfacing (glue side to wrong side of fabric; place-and-lift press)
  • Trace darts onto interfaced fabric (both triangles + horizontal waist line)
  • Sew all fish-eye darts (no backstitch at points — tie off by hand)
  • Press all darts (both halves toward side seam; place-and-lift)
  • Try on pinned jacket, determine welt pocket placement
  • Transfer welt placement symmetrically to both front pieces
  • Sew welt pocket(s)
  • Source shoulder pads (½″ uncovered batting type) and sleeve heads

If Cutting at Home

Take a photo and email the instructor with any questions before you cut. It's much harder to fix a cutting error after the fact.
