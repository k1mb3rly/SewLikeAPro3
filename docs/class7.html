Class 7 Reference Guide — Neck Seam Tack, Buttonhole Marking, Attaching the Lining & Sleeve Lining Finish

March 12, 2026

Based on the class transcript from Sew Like a Pro 3, Spring 2026. This guide covers tacking the neck seam allowances, marking buttonholes with thread tracing, attaching the lining at the upper and lower edges, turning the jacket right side out, attaching the sleeve linings, and hand-tacking the lining in place.

What's Inside

Parts 1 & 2

Neck Seam Tack & Buttonhole Marking

Hand-tacking the collar neck seam allowances together and thread-tracing buttonhole positions onto the jacket.

 Parts 3 & 4

Attaching the Lining

Pinning and sewing the lining to the facing edges, then sewing the bottom hem — including how to handle the hem extension lip.

 Parts 5 & 6

Turning & Sleeve Lining

Turning the jacket right side out through the lining hole, managing the hem overhang, and attaching sleeve linings using the reach-through technique.

 Parts 7 & 8

Hand Tacks & Quick Reference

Tacking the armhole and bottom hem seam allowances to keep the lining in place, plus the full checklist and what's coming next class.
