Blazer Construction Sequence

This is the order of operations for the entire blazer. At this point in class you are working through steps 1–7.

  1. Finish tracing and editing all pattern pieces
  2. Cut all pieces (main fabric, lining, interfacing)
  3. Fuse interfacing to all required pieces
  4. Trace darts onto fabric (if doing darts)
  5. Sew and press all darts
  6. Determine welt pocket placement
  7. Sew welt pocket(s)
  8. Sew body seams (fronts to backs)
  9. Set in sleeves, add sleeve heads & shoulder pads

How to Fuse Interfacing

Place the interfacing glue (dot) side against the wrong side of the fabric. Press firmly to fuse — use a place-and-lift motion, not a sliding iron.

Tip

Interface each piece individually to its cut shape — do NOT block-fuse a large slab and cut afterward. This wastes interfacing and gives uneven results on partial pieces like the back jacket.
