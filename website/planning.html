Part 4: Planning Ahead

Prep for next class and what's coming up.

Next Class Prep

  • If you traced your pattern today: bring your jacket fabric to the next class so you can begin cutting after edits.
  • If you didn't trace today: tracing and editing will happen next class. If you don't anticipate major edits, bring your fabric anyway.
  • You can always cut your fabric at home between classes if needed.
  • Size chart and yardage requirements will be written on the board and are size-dependent.
  • Measuring instructions will be reviewed next class so you can select the correct size.

Construction Sequence for the Actual Blazer

The seams themselves are straightforward (right sides together, press open). The challenging elements of this jacket are:

  • Welt pocket construction
  • Setting in the sleeve
  • Attaching the notch collar (what you practiced today)
  • Attaching the lining

Upcoming Resources

  • Fabric Studies for Fashion Designers mini-course starts next Wednesday (3 sessions). Registration was still open as of class time. Includes a museum study room visit and a seam finishing workshop.
  • Shoulder pads and sleeve heads will be shown in class in upcoming weeks.
