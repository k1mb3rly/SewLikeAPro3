Part 3: Welt Placement & Shoulder Pads

How to find the right pocket position on your body, transfer it symmetrically, and what to source for shoulder pads and sleeve heads.

Determining Welt Pocket Placement

Placement is personal — there is no single "correct" position. The goal is to find a location that flatters your body and avoids unnecessary bulk near the dart.

How to Find Your Position

  1. Pin the front and back jacket pieces together at the side seam and shoulder (right sides out) to simulate the jacket on your body.
  2. Try it on and use your front-facing phone camera to assess where a pocket would sit most naturally.
  3. Mark the desired position with pins or removable chalk.

Transferring the Position Symmetrically

Once you've marked one side, you need to mirror it on the other front piece accurately:

  • Tracing paper method: Place tracing paper over the marked piece and trace the pocket outline, then flip and transfer to the second piece.
  • Push-pin method: Stack both front pieces right sides together, carefully aligned, and push pins through the marked corners to transfer the position to the piece underneath.

Dart & Welt Interaction

If you're adding darts, shift the welt pocket position away from the dart to reduce bulk and construction difficulty. Overlapping a dart and a welt in the same area creates a challenging seam intersection.
